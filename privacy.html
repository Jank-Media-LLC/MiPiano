Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

Introduction

Jank Media LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Mi Piano mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Mi Piano is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Practice session recordings
  • MIDI input data
  • Music files or user content

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Verovio: A music notation rendering engine loaded from the Verovio CDN. This connection is only used to download the rendering library and does not transmit any user data.

Local Storage

All app data, including song selections and preferences, is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties.

MIDI Keyboard Data

When you connect a MIDI keyboard, the App processes note input locally on your device for visual feedback only. No MIDI data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Internet Connection

The App requires an internet connection only to load the Verovio music notation library on first launch. After initial loading, the library is cached for offline use.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from children or any other users. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
